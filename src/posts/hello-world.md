# Hello World!

## Why a Blog?

I've been meaning to write more for a while. Over the years, I've accumulated thoughts on distributed systems, engineering leadership, and building things at scale - and also on a lot of things that have nothing to do with work. I figured it was time to start putting them somewhere permanent.

## What to Expect

I plan to write about:

* **Technical deep-dives** - distributed systems, search infrastructure, large-scale data platforms.
* **Engineering leadership** - how I think about team building, technical strategy, and cross-org alignment.
* **Life outside work** - random things I've learned, observed, or am currently figuring out.
