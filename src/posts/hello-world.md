# Hello World!

Welcome to my blog! This is the first post on my new site.

## Why a Blog?

I've been meaning to write more for a while. Over the years, I've accumulated a lot of thoughts on distributed systems, engineering leadership, and building things at scale — and I figured it was time to start putting them somewhere permanent.

## What to Expect

I plan to write about:

- **Technical deep-dives** — distributed systems, search infrastructure, large-scale data platforms
- **Engineering leadership** — how I think about team building, technical strategy, and cross-org alignment
- **Lessons learned** — things I wish I'd known earlier in my career

## A Bit About Me

I'm a software engineer currently at Meta, where I lead teams working on large-scale search and retrieval systems. Before that I was at Amazon, Walmart/Jet.com, and Microsoft. I've had the chance to work on some fascinating problems across FinTech, supply chain, and productivity tooling.

If any of those topics interest you, stick around — more posts coming soon.
